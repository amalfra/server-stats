## Security

The security of your personal information is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. We request server access credentials, including the server IP address, username, and SSH key, solely for the purpose of connecting to the server. All such information is only used once at time of establishing connection, and is not copied, shared, or disclosed to any third party.
